My Favorite Restaurants in Saipan

In the beautiful island of Saipan, there are plenty of restaurants to explore. In this article, I'll show you my top picks on where to grab a bite in Saipan!

Top Picks

  1. Savory Bistro Cafe
  2. Surf Club Saipan
  3. 360° Restaurant
  4. American Pizza & Grill
  5. J's Restaurant

Savory Bistro Cafe

Savory Bistro Cafe is located in Middle Road, Garapan. It's food consists of Filipino food mixed with a bit of Chamorro food. I love the ambiance and not to mention their karaoke rooms, which is very fun when having parties and gatherings. My favorite is their Savory Fried Chicken. They also serve iced tea and some alcoholic drinks so if you are looking for a place to party and have fun, then I think Savory Bistro Cafe is the place for you!

Best Things About Savory Bistro Cafe:

  • Fantastic ambiance
  • Flavorful food
  • Affordable

Surf Club Saipan

Surf Club Saipan is located in Chalan Kanoa. What I love about this restaurant is the tropical and island theme. The restaurant has a beach area where anyone can eat and feel the island life. They serve different types of food like American, Filipino, and Thai. My favorite is their vegetable lumpia. They also have a bar that has tons of drinks. If you are looking for a place to relax and experience the island life, then Surf Club Saipan is the place for you!

Best Things About Surf Club Saipan:

  • Different types of dishes
  • Tropical atmosphere
  • Excellent customer service

360° Restaurant

360° Restaurant is located in Susupe. The best thing about this restaurant is the breathtaking views because you are actually eating upstairs where the restaurant itself is a rotating carousel. They serve mostly American with a bit of Filipino food. The food tastes great and the customer service is one of the best. In my opinion, this is one of the best restaurants to have a date with your loved ones. If you are looking for a restaurant that has great view of Saipan, then this is the best place for you!

Best Things About 360° Restaurant:

  • Has one of the best views of Saipan
  • Food feels luxurious
  • Friendly customer service

American Pizza & Grill

American Pizza & Grill is located in Garapan. I think they have if not the best pizzas and burgers in Saipan. It's very delicious and the price is definitely worth it. A lot of tourists like to eat here because it has classic American food and the atmosphere has that American touch to it. My favorite is the classic cheese burger and their fried chicken. If you are looking for a place that has American food and an American atmosphere, then American Pizza & Grill is the place for you!

Best Things About American Pizza & Grill:

  • Delicious pizza and burgers
  • Classic American atmosphere
  • Expensive but worth it

J's Restaurant

J's Restaurant has two locations and is located in Gualo Rai and Beach Road. The restaurant might not have one of the best atmospheres but it is very cozy and comfortable to dine in. The food has a style of mainly Filipino and Chamorro. A lot of the locals like to eat here because the food tastes like home and is tasty. In the Gualo Rai location, the restaurant has an arcade area where both kids and adults can play gamees. If you are looking for a place to eat and be comfortable just like home, then this is the place for you!

Best Things About J's Restaurant:

  • Comfortable and homey atmosphere
  • Not too expensive
  • Food is delivered fast
